The term animal refers to a category of mobs and blocks that are mainly based on real life animals and mythical/made-up animals.


== List of Animals ==


=== Breedable ===
These mobs can be directly bred. Majority of them are required for the Two by Two advancement with the Trader Llama only present in the The Parrots and the Bats advancement.‌


=== Tameable ===
These animals can be tamed in different ways. The Best Friends Forever advancement applies to them.‌

Armadillo
Camel
Camel Husk (after the camel husk jockey riders are dismounted)
Cat
Donkey
Horse
Nautilus
Llama
Mule
Parrot
Trader Llama
Wolf
Zombie Horse (after the zombie horseman jockey rider is dismounted)
Zombie Nautilus (after the zombie nautilus jockey rider is dismounted)


=== Arthropod ===
It's considered an arthropod by the Bane of Arthropods enchantment.

Bee
Cave Spider
Endermite
Silverfish
Spider


=== Coral blocks ===
These blocks and their variations such as coral blocks and coral fans are in-game classified as corals.

Brain Coral
Bubble Coral
Fire Coral
Horn Coral
Tube Coral


=== Unclassified ===
These mobs and blocks don't have an in-game category but were simply based on real life animals or mythical animals such as dragons.

Salmon
